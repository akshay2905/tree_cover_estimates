Map Visualisation - Tree Cover Estimates exclusive of Forest Area.

What is the map trying to represent?
=> This map shows the tree cover estimates of the states in India.This data doesnot include the forest area.

What do the color represent ?
=> The color represent the percentage tree cover relative to other states. The darkest shade represent highest percentage of tree cover and the lighter shades represents lowerpercentage of tree cover.

How can I view the information on tree cover of an indivdiual state?
=> You can simply hover(move your mouse over) the state and the information will be displayed on the upper right corner text box.

Are the Union Territories also part of the visualisation?
=>Yes. But as the UT area is pretty small, you will have to zoom in on the areas.
